As a chess player, I will give away some tips I use when playing chess, so that you can try and use it in your games.

  • Control the center early with pawns and pieces
  • Develop your pieces quickly
  • Castle early to keep your king safe
  • Always check for checks, captures, and threats before moving
  • Don't leave pieces undefended or hanging
  • Improve your worst-placed piece instead of only attacking
  • Think about your opponent's plan, not just your own
  • Place rooks on open or semi-open files
  • Don't rush attacks without full development
  • Create weaknesses in your opponent's position
  • Think of multiple candidate moves before choosing one
  • Learn from your mistakes after every game
